# Xads — Business Context for Claude Code

## Who We Are

**Company**: Xads  
**Stage**: Pre-seed, functional prototype, early industry presence  
**Industry**: AdTech — Outdoor Advertising (OOH)  
**Location**: Gurugram, Haryana, India  
**Team**: 3rd-year students at Masters Union, Gurugram  

## What We Do

Xads is building the **"Facebook Ads Manager for Billboard Advertising"** in India.

Outdoor advertising (hoardings, transit ads, digital screens) is a $600M+ industry in India that is still bought/sold like it's 1995 — phone calls, spreadsheets, gut feel. There is zero data analytics, no programmatic buying, no real-time performance tracking.

We are changing this by incubating a **data analytics platform** that gives advertisers:
- Real-time footfall and audience data for outdoor locations
- Campaign performance analytics (impressions, reach, demographics)
- Programmatic buying capabilities for OOH inventory
- A self-serve portal similar to Facebook/Google Ads

## Ideal Competition Profile

A competition is high-priority for us if:
- **Theme fit**: AdTech, data analytics, SaaS, B2B tech, media tech, marketing tech, smart cities
- **Stage fit**: Pre-seed, MVP, prototype, idea-stage (we are NOT Series A ready yet)
- **Open to India**: Indian teams allowed, or international/global
- **Deadline**: Within next 90 days, or rolling applications
- **Prize**: Cash, equity-free funding, mentorship, incubation, or strong brand recognition
- **Organiser credibility**: IIT/IIM/top universities, Fortune 500, top VCs, government (DPIIT, NASSCOM)
